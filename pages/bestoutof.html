Reduce Reuse Recycle! Best out of E-waste

make the best using waste! About the Event

“Electronic recycling is the process of recovering material from old devices to use new products.” The waste generated from any electronic would be used in creating a new idea, design and a product. The highest utility creative based product or a design will be considered for selection.


Know it to break it! Rules:

  • Two participants per team.
  • Use only E-waste items are allowed, any glue can be used to fix the parts.
  • Ensure that no damage is caused around the surrounding.
  • Any number of teams can participate.
  • Creation of the product is left to your choice.
  • All the materials required should be bought by the participants
  • Judge’s decision would be final.
