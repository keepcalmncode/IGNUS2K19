Pictionary

A Picture that talks!

Know it to Break it!

Rules and Regulations:

  • Each team should have 3 members.
  • The picturist will have to pick a card which consist of a word or a phrase. The picturist can take 5 seconds to read and 30 seconds to draw and the teammates have to guess that word/phrase.
  • Two teams will compete against each other where each team will take alternate turns to draw.
  • The game consists of 3 rounds where each member of the team will take turns as the picturist. The winning team gets one point. And the final round is a 1vs1 round where members of both the teams will be given the same card and draw at the same time. The team who wins gets 2 points and the winning team of that round goes to the next round.
