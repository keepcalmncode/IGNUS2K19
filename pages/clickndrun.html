Click as you Run! CLICK & RUN

It's Not Just A Selfie! About the Event

Click and Run is an event where the players try to find hidden parts of the any given object based on the clues given and click a selfie with every clue within a stipulated time and assemble and narrate it.


Know it to break it! Rules:

  • 3 to 4 participants in a team
  • Only one person is allowed to use mobile phone in a team.
  • Any number of teams can participate from a college.
  • Judges decision would be final.
