Mad Libs Theatre

— Left or Right, Laugh or Cry.

About The Event

Each team will be handed over a script with a few words missing.
The participants must fill the blanks with a subsequent answers( like a noun, a weird word, a verb ending with 'ing').



Rules

Each Team should contian 2 participants. Single round and time limit game. Best act will be considered as a winner.

Each script will have an emotion written on it,participants must then enact their scripts with that emotion within a minute.

The registration fee is 50 Rupees per team.
