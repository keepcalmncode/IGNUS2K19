About the Event?

Our treasure hunt is information-based puzzle hunt, in which teams use their collective brain power to solve clues. Each team is presented with puzzles, which teams must solve in order to find the clues. Each clue leads to a location within walking distance, where players must find another clue. This clue could be words, a series of number or an address or any other unique item which are based on technical and non-technical terms. Teams travel through the routes of the playing area running to find the clue. There are totally five rounds, teams have to win in order to qualify for the next round.

Our Rules

Ahoy, Matey | Avast Ye

Rule 1

Each team consists of two members.

Rule 2

Do not open and view the clues prior to the announcement that the "hunt has started".

Rule 3

Teams are noticed by a volunteer.

Rule 4

Team members must stay together and should not communicate through cell phones.

Rule 5

Team members should not take help from others to solve clues.

Rule 6

Teams are allocated to utilize the only resources which are approved by the organizers.

Rule 7

Teams may or may not use smart phones or any internet devices.

Rule 8

Do not displace, tamper or destroy the clue at the respective locations.
