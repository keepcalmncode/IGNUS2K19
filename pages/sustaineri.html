About


Sustainability is a process of maintaining change in a balanced enviorment in which the exploitation of resources , The direction of investments , The orientation of technological development and institutional change are all in harmony and enhance both current and future potential to meet human needs and aspirations.


Sustainability is defined through the interconnected domains or pillars of enviorment economy & society.


Knowledge based on the 3 pillars shall be tested.
